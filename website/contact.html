Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report an issue in the app?

You can report issues directly through the app or by emailing us at support@communityuo.com. Please include details about the issue, your device type, and app version if possible.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account through the app settings, or contact us at support@communityuo.com and we'll assist you with account deletion.

How do I update my email or password?

You can update your email and password directly in the app through the Settings menu.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Use the "Forgot Password" option on the sign-in screen. We'll send you a password reset link via email.

How do I change my notification preferences?

Go to Settings > Notifications in the app to customize which types of reports you want to be notified about.
